The Genius of Boondocks

I don't know anybody who doesn't love them some classic Boondocks! Aaron McGruder came up with the perfect idea and doesn't get enough credit for it. Rottentomatoes.com didn't give the show the love it deserved but at least the audience knew what they had. I want to discuss my favorite characters from the show and after maybe you could register so you don't miss any great nerd content from us!!!

Huey Freeman

Politically sapient and borderline militant, Huey, being a self-described revolutionary left-wing radical, regularly reflects upon current events as well as the plight of African Americans as it relates to a greater American society.

Riley Freeman

He is Huey Freeman's younger brother and Robert Freeman's youngest grandson. He is an 8–year-old who emulates the gangster lifestyle. He and many others commonly refer to him as "Riley Escobar", "Young Reezy", and "The Fundraiser".

Learn More »

Uncle Ruckus

He is a black man who firmly doesn't like black people -- the world's biggest "Uncle Tom". An overweight, horrid, detestable homely man with one oversized glass-eye, he enjoys disassociating himself from other African Americans as much as possible, and is outspoken in his support of what Huey calls the "white supremacist power structure."
